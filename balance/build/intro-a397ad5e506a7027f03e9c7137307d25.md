# Central Bank and Government Transactions

This book collects the transaction diagrams and balance-sheet examples from the
`cb_govt_transactions.ipynb` notebook.

The main notebook walks through:

- starting central bank and government balance sheets
- currency reserves financed by the government
- a gold-price revaluation
- direct central bank lending to government
- government bond issuance purchased by the central bank
- exporting the charts into a single HTML report

```{note}
The original notebook is included as the main computational chapter. Keep the
supporting Python files and output folders next to the notebook when building
the book.
```
